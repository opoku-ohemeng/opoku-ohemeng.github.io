Preforming DoS Attack

This project demonstrates the understanding of the anatomy of a botnet. The role of a command-and-control (C&C / C2) server. Identifying the three primary categories of denial-of-service attacks. Performing a Watering Hole Attack

This project demonstrates how social engineering techniques can be used in conjunction with technical exploits. Identifying the key characteristics of a watering hole attack. Use Metasploit to deploy a malicious payload and gain remote shell access. Use Metasploit to perform post-exploitation operations. Identify the key stages of Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs).
